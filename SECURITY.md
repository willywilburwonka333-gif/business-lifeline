# Business Lifeline Security Policy

## Reporting a vulnerability

Please report suspected security issues privately to **businesslifeline.au@gmail.com** with the subject line `Security report — Business Lifeline`.

Include:

- the affected page, endpoint or feature;
- clear reproduction steps;
- the potential impact;
- screenshots or a minimal proof of concept where safe;
- your preferred contact details.

Do not include passwords, banking credentials, tax file numbers, identity documents, customer records, employee records or confidential contracts.

## Responsible testing boundaries

Good-faith research must not:

- access, alter, delete or publish another person’s data;
- disrupt availability or generate excessive traffic;
- use social engineering, phishing or credential attacks;
- attempt physical access;
- retain sensitive information discovered accidentally;
- publicly disclose an issue before a reasonable remediation period has passed.

Stop testing immediately if personal, financial or confidential business information becomes visible. Report what occurred without copying or distributing the information.

## Response process

Business Lifeline is an Early Access product operated by Wilbur Wonka Studio. We aim to acknowledge a credible report within **5 business days**, assess severity, contain material risk, and provide updates where practical. Response times are targets rather than guarantees.

We may ask for additional information. We will not pursue legal action against good-faith researchers who comply with this policy and applicable law.

## Supported version

Security fixes are applied to the current production version. Preview deployments and historical builds may not receive separate fixes.
